How it works

From runbook to repeatable process

Four steps to turn operational knowledge into a reliable, repeatable system.

1

Import

Paste your existing markdown runbook or create a fresh checklist from scratch.

2

Structure

Organize tasks into sections. Add items, reorder, and refine your process.

3

Execute

Work through your checklist. Progress saves automatically. Pick up where you left off.

4

Iterate

After each cycle, improve your checklist. Operational knowledge compounds over time.
