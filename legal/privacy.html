Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Information we collect

When you create an account, we collect the information you provide directly:

  • Account information — your email address, name, and authentication credentials used to sign in.
  • Profile information — your display name and avatar preferences.
  • Checklist content — the checklists, sections, items, and progress data you create and manage within the application.

We do not collect payment information directly. Any future payment processing will be handled by a third-party payment processor.

How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Quickcheck service.
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account.
  • Sync your checklist data across devices and sessions.
  • Communicate with you about service updates, security notices, and support inquiries.

Third-party services

Quickcheck uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — provides authentication, database storage, and serverless infrastructure. Your account credentials and checklist data are stored in Supabase's infrastructure.
  • Google Sign-In — if you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your email address and basic profile information from Google. No additional Google services are accessed.
  • GitHub Sign-In — if you choose to sign in with GitHub, we receive your email address and basic profile information from GitHub. No additional GitHub services are accessed.

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

Data retention

We retain your account information and checklist data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

Backup copies may persist in encrypted form for up to 90 days before being fully purged.

Data security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Checklist data is stored in a PostgreSQL database with row-level security, ensuring data isolation between accounts.
  • Authentication is handled by Supabase Auth, which uses secure session management and encrypted tokens.

Your rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time through your account settings. If you need assistance, please contact us.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your checklist data for training machine learning models or for any purpose other than providing the service to you.
